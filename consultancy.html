Feasibility with our Testing Facility

  • Maximum of two ports up to and including 40GHz (R & S ZNB 40, 100kHz to 40GHz)
  • Antenna pattern measurements in both the principal plane cuts along with co-pol and cross-pol measurements in the frequency range of 1 to 24GHz (with a far field distance of 4.1 m).

Measurement Details

The customer must provide the following details:

  1. Principal plane cuts for antenna pattern measurements (XY or YZ or XZ)
  2. Co-polarization / Cross polarization
  3. Orientation of the antenna with respect to the standard gain test horn antenna
  4. Number of frequency points
  5. Frequency sweeps as applicable
